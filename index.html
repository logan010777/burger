Cheese burger

A cheeseburger is a popular type of sandwich consisting of a juicy
beef patty, typically cooked to perfection on a grill or griddle,
served on a soft bun with melted cheese, fresh lettuce, juicy tomato,
and tangy pickles. It is a classic American fast food staple that has
become a favorite around the world.
