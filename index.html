Cheese burger

A cheeseburger is a popular type of sandwich consisting of a juicy
beef patty, typically cooked to perfection on a grill or griddle,
served on a soft bun with melted cheese, fresh lettuce, juicy tomato,
and tangy pickles. It is a classic American fast food staple that has
become a favorite around the world.

Bacon Cheeseburger

A bacon cheeseburger is a savory sandwich made with a beef patty,
melted cheese, and crispy bacon, typically served on a bun.

Patty Melt

A patty melt is a grilled sandwich made with a beef patty, melted cheese, and caramelized onions, served on toasted bread.

BBQ Burger

A BBQ burger is a delicious sandwich made with a grilled beef patty , topped with BBQ sauce, melted cheese, and sometimes bacon, served on a bun.

Salmon Burger

A salmon burger is a healthy and flavorful alternative to a beef burger, made with a patty of ground salmon, herbs, and spices, typically served on a bun with toppings such as avocado, lettuce, and mayonnaise.

Blue Cheese Burger

A blue cheese burger is a tasty sandwich made with a beef patty, topped with creamy blue cheese, and often accompanied by toppings such as bacon, onions, or mushrooms, served on a bun
